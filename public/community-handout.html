Weekly Care Checklist

Cut this page out and hang it on your wall near your grow area

DAILY — 2 to 3 minutes

Check water level — top off if low
Test pH — target 5.8 to 6.2
Look at plants for wilting or bugs
Make sure light timer is working

WEEKLY — 10 to 15 minutes

Measure nutrient strength (EC/TDS meter)
Clean any green slime (algae)
Check roots — white is healthy, brown is bad
Make sure air pump is bubbling in each bucket
Harvest any crops that are ready

EVERY 2 WEEKS — 20 to 30 minutes

Drain all buckets and refill with fresh nutrient water
Scrub inside of buckets clean
Trim tangled roots if needed
Check all tubes and connections
Reminder: Add Micro FIRST, stir, then Gro, stir, then Bloom

MONTHLY — 30 minutes

Calibrate your pH and EC meters
Check air stones — replace if clogged
Raise light as plants grow taller
Clean fan blades
Check supplies — reorder before running out

Troubleshooting — Quick Fixes

If something looks wrong, find it below and follow the fix

Yellow leaves on bottom
Cause: Not enough nutrients
Fix: Add more nutrients or check pH
Brown tips on leaves
Cause: Too much nutrients (burn)
Fix: Add plain water to dilute
Plant drooping even with water
Cause: Root rot — roots are brown and slimy
Fix: Check roots. Keep water temp below 75 F
Plant growing very slow
Cause: pH is wrong
Fix: Adjust pH to 5.8 to 6.2 range
Green slime in bucket
Cause: Light is getting into the water
Fix: Cover any gaps. Use dark/opaque buckets
Tiny flies buzzing around
Cause: Fungus gnats
Fix: Keep top of clay pebbles dry. Use yellow sticky traps
Plant is tall and skinny (leggy)
Cause: Not enough light
Fix: Lower the light closer to plants
Leaf edges curling up
Cause: Too hot or too much wind
Fix: Move fan away. Keep temp 65 to 80 F

Healthy roots are WHITE

If roots are brown or slimy, that is the first thing to fix
